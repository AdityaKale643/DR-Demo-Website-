# 🎨 Color Palette Guide

Choose the perfect color scheme for your medical practice!

## How to Use This Guide

1. Find the specialty that matches your practice
2. Copy the color codes
3. Open `styles.css`
4. Replace lines 2-7 with your chosen colors
5. Save and refresh your browser

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Always test on mobile** - Colors may look different
2. **Use contrast checker** - Ensure text is readable
3. **Consider your patients** - Age groups prefer different colors
4. **Be consistent** - Keep your branding consistent
5. **Trust is key** - Blue and green are most trusted in healthcare

### Accessibility Tips:
- Ensure at least 4.5:1 contrast ratio for text
- Avoid red-green only combinations
- Test with color blindness simulator
- Check: webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker
